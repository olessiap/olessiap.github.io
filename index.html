GMP

Good Manufacturing Practice

  • According to the World Health Organization, GMP is a system for ensuring that products are consistently produced according to quality standards
  • GMP is the proactive part of quality assurance
  • It is designed to minimize the risks involved in all steps of the manufacturing process
  • A basic tenet of GMP is that quality cannot be tested into a product. It must be built into each batch of product during all stages of the manufacturing process
  • GMP involves much more than what most people think. A common misconception is that GMP only covers the process of manufacturing itself

GMP covers all aspects of the production process

materials premises equipment storage record keeping staff training hygiene product development how complaints are handled
